Style Guide

"Content is king."

Purpose

Why is this being created? Is it for informational purposes? To increase awareness of an event? To result in conversions?

Audience

Who is this for? What demographics do they fit into and what are their interests?

Tone

What feelings should this evoke? Should it come accross as simple and straightforward? Informative and reliable? Complex and sophisticated?

Preferences

Are there any websites that you particularly like or dislike? What about it/them do you like/dislike? Is there anything else I should know before drawing up a first draft?
